# Atlas

Atlas is an AI-powered workplace simulation platform that helps people gain real professional experience before their first day on the job.

## Vision

We believe every professional deserves the chance to build professional memory before their first day on the job.

## What Atlas does

- AI workplace simulations
- Realistic stakeholders with memory
- AI mentor and feedback
- Inbox, meetings, tasks and documents
- Decision-based learning
- Progress tracking
